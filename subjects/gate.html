KUUMAT stands for Kathmandu University Undergraduate Management Admission Test.

It is the first phase of admission test for admission into KU’s undergraduate programs (BBA,BBIS and BHTM). After Passing KUUMAT you will be eligible to apply for admission in KUSOM and affiliated colleges of KU.

This test is for those who want to pursue their higher studies in BBA, BBIS and BHTM.

Note: Applicants who have completed the qualifying educational level but are awaiting the final results may apply on a provisional basis,

provided he/she has already taken final annual examination of the program and cleared all previous year papers.

Those applying on a provisional basis should have secured minimum 50 percent or CGPA of 2 in his/her 11th grade annual exam.
